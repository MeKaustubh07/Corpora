# Atlas Robotics — Internal Handbook (fictional eval corpus)

## Company

Atlas Robotics was founded in 2019 in Pune by Meera Iyer and Daniel Okafor.
The company builds autonomous warehouse robots. Headcount reached 240
employees in January 2026.

## Products

The flagship product is the AR-7 picker robot, launched in November 2023.
The AR-7 carries payloads up to 18 kilograms and runs 14 hours per charge.
A cheaper model, the AR-3, launched in June 2025 with a 6 kilogram payload
limit and is aimed at small warehouses.

## Pricing

The AR-7 costs 42,000 dollars per unit. The AR-3 costs 15,500 dollars.
Volume discounts start at 10 units: 8 percent off list price. Annual
maintenance contracts cost 3,000 dollars per robot.

## Support policy

Standard support hours are 9am to 6pm IST, Monday through Saturday.
Premium support (24/7) is included free for fleets of 25 or more robots;
otherwise it costs 9,000 dollars per year. Hardware faults are repaired
on-site within 48 hours in metro cities.

## Safety

Every robot performs a self-diagnostic at boot. If lidar calibration drifts
beyond 2 degrees, the robot halts and reports error code E-114. Emergency
stop buttons are located on both the front and rear panels.
